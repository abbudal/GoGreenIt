Advantages of going LED

    LED
  • 85 % less than incandescent
  • 50 % less than CFL
  • 25,000 – 50,000 hrs
  • Glass or Plastic enclosure shatter proof
  • No mercury
  • Works on all temperatures
  • Insusceptible to humidity

Why switch to LED technology?

LED technology is high efficiency lighting technology that saves you energy and lowers your electricity bills tremendously. With an extended lifespan up to 50,000 hrs LED’s reduce your labor cost for replacing new lights therefore saving you additional money that can be better used to grow your business.
